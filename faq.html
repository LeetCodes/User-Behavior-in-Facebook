Facebook Privacy Analyzer & Watcher Research

What are FPA and FPW?


It's a browser extension (Chrome,Firefox)

FPA: Facebook Privacy Analyzer

used to collect data about facebook users (who want to participate).

FPW: Facebook Privacy Watcher

Used to help user to customize its privacy settings on Facebook via C4PS.

What do we collect about you?


We will collect about you:

  • Your Friend IDs.
  • Your Activity Log.
  • Information provided in "About" section in your profile.
  • Liked pages and joined groups.
  • Session time and the time you will spend on posts.

    • What is C4PS?


    Colors for Privacy Settings, it provides easy access to your privacy settings.

    It uses colors to help you adjusting posts on Facebook. i.e:

  • Green: Public Post (Visible to every one).
  • Yellow: Friends.
  • Blue: Cutom Post (Selected Friends).
  • Red: Only me.

    • Why this Research?


    Its a renewing of previous study to examine user behaviour on Facebook.

    What happend to the collected Data?


    Your data will be accumulated with other participants data, then it will be analyzed to obtain user behavior. Anonymization is to be considered.

    How Anonymization is to be taken into account?


    Your data will be collected without your identity. i.e: we know that someone clicked like, but whom? we don't know.
